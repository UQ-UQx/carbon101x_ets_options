Learn to Select Optimal ETS Options

In this ungraded practical, you will learn how to select optimal ETS options and projects in order to achieve the lowest possible Average Cost/CO2e and not incur a penalty cost.

Enerco's Scope 1 carbon footprint in Year 1 is 3,500,000 tCO2e. The Tasland Government has given you 1,200,000 free allowances. Thus, your remaining liability is 2,300,000 tCO2e. You must purchase one (1) allowance or offset for every 1 tCO2e of your company's liability, otherwise the government will fine you a penalty price ($50) for every tCO2e of your shortfall.

Instructions:

  1. Drag and re-order projects by the ascending (i.e., lowest to highest) price.
  2. Select the cheapest projects/options by clicking on the checkbox next to the project/option name until the compliance obligation is met. The project will be added to the Volume and Cost graph to help you visualise if compliance has been met. The Penalty Cost and Average $/tCO2e are also displayed. Your goal is to achieve a Penalty Cost of 0.
  3. If a project is selected, the abatement volume will be fixed.
  4. If an ETS option is selected, you will need to decide on the amount to purchase, a limit may however apply. For cheaper ETS options, it is useful to purchase the maximum amount available.
